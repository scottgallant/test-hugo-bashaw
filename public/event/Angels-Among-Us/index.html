December 12th: Angel Fest

Event Date December 12th

This November, strap on your wings and join the movement as Bashaw United Church challenges the community to perform more than 100 random Acts of Angels in just 40 days! From Nov 1st to Dec 12th community members are being challenged to catch others in the act of doing good or spreading love, and report those Acts of Angels by using the "Angel Cards" found around town at various locations, or by using the hashtag #BashawAngels on social media. We will be collecting those Angels Among Us stories and sharing them with the community through Facebook, and also by adding them to the Angels Among Us Exhibit - a collection of stories posted on laminated Angel cards on the front lawn of the Bashaw United Church.

The Facebook Page "Angels Among Us" has already reached tens of thousands of people in the last 2 weeks, and the angels on the lawn of the church are quickly starting to multiply! And we know this is only going to continue growing, with your support. The Angels Among Us challenge wraps up on December 12th with "Angel Fest"; a fun, family festival featuring a full day of events for community members of all ages. Check out the schedule of events and mark your calendars!

Schedule

8:30 a.m. Check In Angel Fun Run & Walk - Registration is $25 (preschoolers FREE)
10:00 a.m. Start Strap on your wings, shine up your halo and make your way through our community angel route. Prizes for top pledge earners and a souvenir toque for every participant.
10am - 3pm Silent Angel Auction
Angel themed silent auction items on display for bidding all day.
10am - 3pm Angel Photo Booth
Bring your friends and family and pose together in our angel photo booth after finding the perfect angel gear in our costume trunk!
10am - 3pm Angel Food Bake Sale
Stock up on some heavenly Christmas goodies at our special Angel Food Bake Sale.
4:00 p.m. Angels Assemble!
Take a seat and watch the cutest actors you’ve ever seen take the stage in this angel themed Christmas theatre production.
5:00 p.m. Angel Hair Spaghetti Supper
End the day with one final stroll through the silent auction items at the church and warm up with a delicious noodle and sauce extravaganza!
